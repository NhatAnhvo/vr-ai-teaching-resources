Use the questions below to find applications aligned with your instructional needs:

Are you introducing students to VR for the first time?
  • Meta First Contact – A playful, guided intro to VR with a robot companion.
  • Bodyswaps – Soft skills simulations with reflective guidance.
  • Virti – Low-barrier entry to conversational VR via AI avatars.
Do you want students to explore real-world places or experiences?
  • Wander – Virtual tourism to global landmarks via Street View.
  • Mission: ISS – Simulate life and tasks aboard the ISS.
  • Anne Frank House – Step into a historically accurate WWII hiding place.
Are you teaching empathy or storytelling through lived experiences?
  • We Live Here – Explore homelessness through memory objects and narration.
  • Now Is the Time – Engage with themes from MLK’s modern march.
  • Notes on Blindness – Understand blindness through immersive audio diaries.
Do your students need hands-on or vocational training?
  • Skillveri Workshop – Practice welding, painting, and HVAC skills in VR.
  • MadXR – Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedure simulations.
  • Risk Hunt – Find workplace hazards in a virtual factory.
  • PNX Labs 3D Printer – Safely explore industrial 3D printing workflows.
Do you want to enhance academic writing, grading, or research support with AI?
  • ChatGPT – Provide writing help, feedback, or content generation.
  • Gradescope – AI-assisted grading for exams and assignments.
  • NotebookLM – Organize and analyze readings and research.
  • Examind – Create and evaluate AI-generated assessments.
Are you designing new experiences or prototypes in XR?
  • ShapesXR – Collaborate on 3D ideas and immersive storyboards.
  • Captivatar – Build branded 360° and XR promotional experiences.
  • SynergyXR – Host live training and team-based VR spaces.
